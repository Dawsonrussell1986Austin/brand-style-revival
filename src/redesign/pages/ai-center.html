Our approach

Three pillars. One vision for AI-ready schools.

The ACES Center for AI helps schools and districts build the knowledge, systems, and structures needed to use artificial intelligence responsibly and effectively. Our work is organized around three interconnected pillars that support educators, leaders, and students at every stage of implementation.

Pillar 1

AI Literacy & Learning

Helping educators and students develop the knowledge, skills, and habits needed to use AI thoughtfully, effectively, and responsibly. This pillar includes professional learning, curriculum, student AI literacy, and workforce readiness.

Explore this pillar
Pillar 2

Innovation & Design

Supporting schools and districts in identifying challenges, designing solutions, improving workflows, and leveraging AI to strengthen teaching, learning, leadership, and organizational effectiveness.

Explore this pillar
Pillar 3

Research & Ethical Standards

Providing the frameworks, guidance, and safeguards needed for responsible AI implementation through governance, policy, ethics, risk management, and evidence-based decision-making.

Explore this pillar

PDSI Mission

ACES Professional Development and School Improvement (PDSI) seeks to deliver high-quality, responsive, equity-centered professional learning that empowers educators to innovate and foster the holistic growth of students. Through partnerships with educational leaders and communities, we aim to build joyful, data-informed, forward-thinking environments that ensure equitable outcomes and learning experiences where educators thrive so that their learners can achieve.
