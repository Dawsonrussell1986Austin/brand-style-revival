AI Literacy & Learning

Helping educators and students develop the knowledge, skills, and habits needed to use AI thoughtfully, effectively, and responsibly.

Our belief

Building AI-Ready Learning Communities.

AI literacy is not just for students. It is essential for educators, school leaders, and district teams who are preparing learners for a rapidly changing world. At the ACES Center for AI, we believe AI literacy is developed through shared learning, thoughtful implementation, and a commitment to keeping human thinking at the center of every decision.

Our work helps schools build the knowledge, instructional practices, and leadership capacity needed to integrate AI responsibly across classrooms, schools, and districts.

Our north star

Our Vision for an AI-Ready Graduate.

Every learning experience developed by the ACES Center for AI is grounded in a shared vision of the knowledge, skills, and dispositions students need to thrive in an AI-enabled world. While AI technologies will continue to evolve, the ability to think critically, exercise sound judgment, communicate effectively, and lead responsibly will remain essential. Our AI literacy work is designed to cultivate these enduring capacities while ensuring that human thinking remains primary.

PDSI Mission

ACES Professional Development and School Improvement (PDSI) seeks to deliver high-quality, responsive, equity-centered professional learning that empowers educators to innovate and foster the holistic growth of students. Through partnerships with educational leaders and communities, we aim to build joyful, data-informed, forward-thinking environments that ensure equitable outcomes and learning experiences where educators thrive so that their learners can achieve.
