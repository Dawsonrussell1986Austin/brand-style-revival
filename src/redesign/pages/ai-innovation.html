Innovation & Design

Supporting schools and districts in identifying challenges, designing solutions, and leveraging AI to strengthen teaching, learning, leadership, and organizational effectiveness.

Our philosophy

Human-Centered Innovation.

Innovation in education is not about replacing people with technology. It is about designing tools that reduce administrative burden, strengthen instructional planning, support better decision-making, and create more time for meaningful teaching and learning. Every solution developed by the ACES Center for AI is grounded in educational research, responsible AI practices, and the belief that human expertise should always guide the work.

Custom AI Solutions for Your District.

Every district has unique goals, systems, and challenges. In addition to our existing AI tools and resources, the ACES Center for AI partners with schools and districts to design customized AI solutions that address local needs and accelerate innovation.

Whether you're exploring a new instructional tool, building district-specific resources, streamlining workflows, or developing an entirely new AI application, our team can help bring your vision to life through collaborative design and development.

Examples might include

  • →Custom AI assistants for educators or leaders
  • →District-specific curriculum and instructional resources
  • →AI-powered workflow and productivity tools
  • →Student learning experiences and applications
  • →Strategic planning and innovation initiatives

PDSI Mission

ACES Professional Development and School Improvement (PDSI) seeks to deliver high-quality, responsive, equity-centered professional learning that empowers educators to innovate and foster the holistic growth of students. Through partnerships with educational leaders and communities, we aim to build joyful, data-informed, forward-thinking environments that ensure equitable outcomes and learning experiences where educators thrive so that their learners can achieve.
