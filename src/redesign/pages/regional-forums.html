PDSI Services · Regional Forums

Educators learning alongside one another.

Regional Forums bring together educators and leaders from districts across Connecticut to share practice, solve common challenges, and learn from one another — together.

The Model

Cross-district learning that lasts.

Rather than one-off workshops, our forums create ongoing space for educators to think deeply about practice while learning from districts experiencing similar successes and challenges. Participants engage in cycles of learning, implementation, reflection, and refinement alongside colleagues from other communities.

  • Job-embedded, collaborative professional learning
  • Classroom visits and shared analysis of practice
  • Honest reflection, problem-solving, and collective learning
