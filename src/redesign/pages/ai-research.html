Why it matters

Building Trust in AI.

Artificial intelligence is transforming education at an unprecedented pace, creating new opportunities for teaching, learning, leadership, and school operations. As AI becomes increasingly integrated into classrooms and district systems, schools need more than access to new technologies. They need trusted guidance that helps them make informed, responsible decisions about how AI is selected, implemented, and evaluated.

At the ACES Center for AI, we help schools build the leadership, governance, and decision-making structures needed to navigate this rapidly evolving landscape with confidence. Through applied research, ethical frameworks, strategic planning, and collaborative partnerships, we support districts in implementing AI in ways that are innovative, transparent, and centered on the needs of students, educators, and communities.

Featured Resource

Designing Assessments in an AI-Influenced World

As artificial intelligence becomes part of everyday teaching and learning, educators need new ways to think about assessment. Rather than focusing on whether AI is allowed or prohibited, this framework helps educators begin with the purpose of the assessment, the evidence of learning that matters most, and the thinking that should remain visible and human.

Designed as a conversation tool rather than a compliance checklist, the framework supports thoughtful assessment design that promotes meaningful learning while recognizing the evolving role of AI in education.

AI Seal of Approval.

Setting the Standard for Responsible AI in Education

As artificial intelligence becomes increasingly integrated into teaching and learning, school leaders need confidence that the tools they adopt are safe, effective, and aligned with educational priorities. The ACES Center for AI is developing the AI Seal of Approval, a comprehensive evaluation and recognition process designed to help districts identify AI tools that meet rigorous standards for educational quality, responsible AI practices, and implementation readiness.

The Seal of Approval will provide schools with a trusted framework for evaluating AI technologies through an educational lens, considering factors such as instructional value, privacy and security, accessibility, transparency, ethical design, and alignment with the needs of students and educators.

Whether districts are exploring new AI platforms or vendors are seeking independent educational review, the AI Seal of Approval will serve as a mark of quality, trust, and responsible innovation.
