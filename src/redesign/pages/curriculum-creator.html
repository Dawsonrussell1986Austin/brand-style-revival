Curriculum Creator.

An AI-assisted platform that helps educators design standards-aligned, culturally responsive curriculum — from course set up to export — in a fraction of the time.

What it is

Curriculum design, guided by AI.

Curriculum Creator walks educators through building complete courses — units, lessons, assessments, and supports — while keeping every generation grounded in your standards, your district's vision, and your students. The more context you provide, the more relevant and responsive the output.

The Course Set Up screen — context entered here informs every AI generation that follows.

Built on trusted frameworks

Three frameworks, working together.

Every course is shaped by three research-based frameworks running in the background — so what you build is compliant, intentionally designed, and accessible to all learners.

CSDE
CT State Dept. of Education

Standards & Compliance

Uses your standards and district context to ensure every unit and lesson aligns with Connecticut requirements.

UbD
Understanding by Design

Backward Design

Uses your challenges and standards to generate backward-designed transfer goals, essential questions, and assessments.

UDL
Universal Design for Learning

Access for All Learners

Uses your student population to generate specific supports tied to CAST UDL checkpoints.

How it works

From set up to export.

A guided workflow takes you through every stage of curriculum development, with AI support at each step.

1

Set Up

Enter course basics, district values, student population, and key standards to ground every generation.

2

Units

Generate backward-designed units with transfer goals, essential questions, and enduring understandings.

3

Lessons

Build standards-aligned lessons with activities, materials, and built-in UDL supports.

4

Audit

Check alignment, rigor, and accessibility against CSDE, UbD, and UDL before you finalize.

5

Finalize

Review, refine, and lock in your course with everything aligned and review-ready.

6

Export

Export polished, shareable curriculum documents ready for teams, leadership, and the classroom.
